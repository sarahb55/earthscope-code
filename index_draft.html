Volcano Island is a pilot project to develop develop a high-quality, interactive computer game to engage and enlighten young people, particularly in the middle-school age group, about EarthScope-related science, people, and projects. cooperation with the EarthScope education and outreach community, the CfA Space Geodesy group and the CfA Science Education department developed Volcano Island.
